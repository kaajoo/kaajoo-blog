Waheguru ji ka khalsa
Waheguru ji ki fateh


Dear Gurmukh Devotees,

We (The Nepalese Sikh community) hereby plea at your feet’s stake regarding the completion of The new Gurudwara Sahib at Birgunj. To everyone’s knowledge it is clear that Birgunj is the second major city after the capital Kathmandu. Birgunj is the core junction of Nepal’s industries, trade and agriculture. Relating the past there were about 500 Sikh families here at birgunj but now they have been reduced to few countable digits. The reasons behind this act are political disturbances, lack of social security, lack of Sikh rights and lack of better upliftment. We feel that this is a loss to the nation and an extinction of our religion from this nation. So we are dedicated to the process of controlling this and re-instating Sikh mass. For this the new Gurudwara that is under construction and is in need of some financial support will be a great platform and a breakthrough for our religion and community.


Therefore we again plea at your feet’s stake to help us achieve our goal and contribute at least 10% of your income to the gurmukh community for its betterment i.e. for the new gurudwara which is under construction. Your contribution will bring us close to our ambition. Our community is clearly excited and eager to see this project succeed. You can be a part of this excitement by supporting us with a donation. We are happy to acknowledge you with this guru’s service and guru’s pride.


Thanking you.

Long live khalsa!!
